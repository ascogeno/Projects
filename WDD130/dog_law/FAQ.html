What's the story of Golden and Murdog?

Originally Feynine and Co Law Offices, Mia Feynine was tragically murdered at the hands of another she was investigating for blackmail. Former subordinate Nick Golden took up the case defending their sister of the crime, and has since taken over the law office. From then it's grown to what it is today!

What's Inusuke's story? Who's he?

Inusuke was a great Ancestor of Nick Golden, origninally from Japan. Life certainly took some turns for the young dog, his field of study was English initially, not law!
After being accused of a murder he didn't commit, he succesfully defended himself in court. The victim was British, hence his trial was under severe scrutiny by both the British and Japanese governments. His friend brought him in secret on a steamship to Britain on a study tour, only to tragically lose his life on that very boat. The study tour was predicated on a lawyer being section to Britain from Japan, so Inusuke took the remainder of the 4 month voyage to study what he could to take his dear friends place.
Inusuke would then go on to establish themselves as one of the greatest barristers London had seen since the turn of the century
